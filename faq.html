General Info.

Mechatron Services specialize in designing and delivering advanced home solutions, including curtains, blinds, wallworks, and flooring, tailored to meet both aesthetic and functional requirements.

About our products

Curtains and Blinds are essential home décor elements that provide privacy, light control, and aesthetic enhancement to your spaces. Our products come in a variety of styles, materials, and custom designs to match your interior theme perfectly.

Wallworks include decorative wall treatments like textured finishes, paneling, murals, and feature walls that transform ordinary spaces into stunning focal points.

Flooring encompasses a wide range of materials, including hardwood, laminate, vinyl, and tiles, designed to enhance the durability and aesthetics of your floors. Our flooring solutions cater to both functional and stylistic needs.
